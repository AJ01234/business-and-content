v1 (July 2026). Warm-list names section to be completed from interview (Known Gap #2).

## Channel strategy (evidence-based)
1. **Warm re-activation (Week 1):** every past client and referrer. Two of them created
   demand RECENTLY without being asked: Dipankar returned with a new song (~2 weeks ago);
   Reshma pitched collaboration 2–3 times. The aesthetic-clinic manager once tried to hire
   him.
2. **In-person pitching in Kanpur (Month 1 onward):** 10 in-person pitches/month. Learned
   at Desire Edits: doctors don't convert on calls — they need physical presence and trust.
   Cold calls only to set up in-person meetings, not to close.
3. **Referral asks:** every delivered project ends with a direct referral request (his
   entire client history is referral-driven — this is the proven channel, so it gets
   systematized, not left to chance).
4. **Content inbound (bonus, never the plan):** HybridBanda documents the work; every
   enquiry gets source-tagged to test whether content produces business (Known Gap #6).

## Definitions
- **Qualified conversation:** decision-maker + discussing their content/leads + past-spend
  question asked + concrete next step proposed. A WhatsApp "hi" does not count.
- **Discovery script core question:** "What did you spend on marketing/content last
  quarter? Who did it? What did you get?" — past behaviour, never "would you buy?"

## Targets (from goal.md)
- 10 in-person pitches/month from Month 1; 20 qualified conversations/month by Month 3
- First paid retainer at proper price by Day 30
- Weekly scorecard every Sunday: pitches held, proposals sent, follow-ups done.
  3 zero-weeks = mandatory diagnosis (goal.md Decision Rule 2)
