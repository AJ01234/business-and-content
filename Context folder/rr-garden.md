## The build (weeks 3–6, inside the cap)

tracking. One build, four jobs: satisfies family, produces the baseline, demos the
growth-systems upsell, generates the first before/after case study.

Father's stated ask (videos + Meta Ads for Kanpur/Lucknow) maps cleanly onto this:
1. Content: venue videos through the RR Garden page (client-work bar, not HybridBanda bar)
