**Written:** July 11, 2026 · **Deadline:** July 11, 2027 · **Review:** weekly numbers, 90-day strategy review

## The goal in one sentence

By July 11, 2027, I earn **₹1,00,000/month in personal take-home income, held for 3
consecutive months**, by selling **productized video retainers (short-form editing + AI
video) at proper prices** to expert-led local businesses — and I earn the right to sell the
bigger "content-to-conversion growth system" only after proving it on my first 1–2 retainer
clients.

## 1. Outcome

I build a small, profitable service business with this structure:

- **Entry offer (sell from Day 1):** a productized monthly video retainer — short-form
  content + AI-assisted video — for expert-led local businesses (doctors/clinics, real
  estate professionals, and the buyer types who have already paid me).
  This is the ONLY thing I sell until it produces reliable cash.
- **Upsell (earn the right):** lead tracking, follow-up, CRM improvement, and reporting —
  the "content-to-conversion system" — offered only to existing retainer clients after I
  have measurably improved results for at least one of them. **Nobody has ever paid me for
  this; I do not lead with it.**
- **Long-term differentiator (portfolio track, not a business yet):** AI films and branded
  visual storytelling. Max 2–3 spec pieces per quarter, built from real client work where
  possible.
- **Distribution layer:** HybridBanda documents the journey (2 short videos/week). Content
  is proof and distribution — never a substitute for outreach or delivery, and inbound from
  content is treated as a bonus, not a plan.

**Why this shape:** my only proven, paid, *returning* demand is video creation — a real
estate marketer paid ₹15–20K/month for ~25–30 videos, and a devotional-music client pays
₹5–7K/video and returned two weeks ago with new work. Every past client came via referral.
The growth-systems idea came from analysis, not from anyone paying for it.

## 2. Number

**Derivation (not aspiration):** my living costs are ~₹15–25K/month and I have under 6
months of runway. So:

- **Survival floor:** ₹25,000/month — must be covered by client revenue by **Day 90** or
  the plan is failing (see decision rules).
- **12-month target:** ₹1,00,000/month personal take-home (≈4–5× living costs), held for
  **3 consecutive months** before July 11, 2027. Requires roughly ₹1.3–1.6L monthly
  collected revenue. The ₹3,00,000 figure from the old file is deleted — it was picked, not
  derived.

**Milestone ladder (runway-driven):**

**Activity numbers (what I control):**

- **10 in-person pitches/month** in Kanpur (doctors buy on physical trust — I learned this
  doing cold calls at Desire Edits), plus warm re-activation and referral asks, ramping to
  **20 qualified conversations/month** by Month 3.
- A **qualified conversation** = a decision-maker, discussing their content/leads, where I
  ask what they currently spend and propose a concrete next step. A WhatsApp "hi" is not a
  conversation.
- 2 HybridBanda short videos/week; 1 long-form/month if sustainable.
- **Price floor:** no project below ₹5,000; no retainer below ₹15,000/month. My history
  (₹800 banners, half-paid edits, underpriced everything) makes this a written rule, not a
  vibe.

## 3. Deadline

**July 11, 2027.** The goal counts as achieved only when ₹1,00,000/month take-home has been
maintained for 3 consecutive months. Interim deadlines above are binding checkpoints, not
suggestions.

## 4. Constraints

- **38–40 focused hours/week:** ~20h selling + delivery, 8h protected HybridBanda time,
  6–8h RR Garden (capped, see below), 4h admin/review/learning. (The old file's 25/35/40
  contradiction is resolved: no job, family expects guest-house help, so ~40 is honest.)
- **Cash budget capped at ₹30,000.** No new equipment; phone, laptop, existing tools.
- **No full-time hires.** Contractors (including the Desire Edits team) only against paid
  client work, costs built into the client's price — after their terms are in writing (see
  Known Gaps #4).
- **No software products** before someone pays for the underlying problem. No new social
  pages before the offer has 3–5 paying customers.
- **Selling before building:** max 7 days on any offer/asset before pitching it to real
  prospects. Learning capped at 3h/week and only for the next deliverable.
- **RR Garden is Client Zero, time-boxed — not a second goal.** My family expects me to
  help run and expand the guest house side by side, so it gets a hard cap of **6–8
  hours/week**, and every one of those hours must run through the systems I sell (enquiry
  log, follow-up cadence, content workflow) so they double as delivery practice and my
  first case study. Scope, authority, and compensation-if-profit-grows still get agreed in
  writing by Day 30. If RR Garden demands exceed the cap for 3 consecutive weeks, I
  renegotiate scope with my father instead of silently absorbing it. The paint shop gets
  nothing this year.
- **No niche/offer changes** based on one rejection or one bad week. Offer review only
  after every 20 qualified conversations, using recorded objections.

## What this goal is NOT (deleted on purpose)

- Not ₹3,00,000/month — a number I admitted I picked, not derived.
- Not "turn RR Garden into a professionally operated business" as a co-equal goal — it is
  Client Zero inside a 6–8h/week cap; helping the family is real, but it rides on my
  systems instead of competing with them.
- Not a wedding-media agency — revisit only if RR Garden opt-in happens and ≥5 packages sell.
- Not an AI-films studio — portfolio track until a client pays for one.
- Not a follower goal — HybridBanda success = published consistency + inbound enquiries,
  not subscriber counts.
- Not "clinics only" — clinics are the lead hypothesis for in-person pitching, but my paid
  history includes real estate and creators/experts; the first 30 conversations decide the
  narrow niche, not the document.

## Decision rules

1. **Runway rule:** if cash on hand drops below 2 months of expenses, I take paid work
   (freelance/part-time) immediately and rebuild from there. No shame spiral, no denial.
2. **Day-90 gate:** if I have zero paying retainer clients at proper prices by Day 90, the
   problem is diagnosed from data (not mood): too few pitches held? pitched but not closed?
   closed but underpriced? Fix the named failure; only change direction after 20+ recorded
   conversations say the offer is wrong.
3. **Upsell gate:** growth-systems work is only pitched to a business after my retainer has
   run there ≥1 month and I can show a number that improved.
4. **Content guardrail:** if I miss both weekly videos for 3 consecutive weeks, I reduce
   scope elsewhere — content hours are protected, but content never excuses skipping
   outreach.
5. **One experiment at a time.** New business ideas get written down and ignored for 90
   days unless they directly serve the current offer.
