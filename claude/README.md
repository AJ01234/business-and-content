## Maintenance rules
- One topic per file. Update the file when a fact changes; date significant updates.
- UNVERIFIED assumptions are labelled as such (finances.md is the big one) — replacing
  them with verified numbers is always high-priority work.
- After every 20 qualified sales conversations: revisit offer.md, client-icp.md,
  market-pricing.md against recorded objections (goal.md review rule).
- The archive `../# 12-Month Goal.md` and `../Master_Context.md` are history, not
  operating documents — mine them for context, don't obey them.
