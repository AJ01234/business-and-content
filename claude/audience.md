# Audience — Who HybridBanda is for, and what it is NOT

Confirmed by Atishay, July 2026.

## The one viewer (write videos for this person)
**An aspiring creator/builder, 20–30**, mostly Indian, tier-1/2/3 cities, who wants to
build a business or become a creator. They watch build-in-public journeys and
self-improvement content. They are self-aware and ambitious — they KNOW what they should be
doing but struggle to consistently do it (Master_Context psychology). They open YouTube/
Instagram hoping to feel: "someone like me, from a place like mine, is actually doing it —
so it's possible, and here's what it really looks like."

They follow people, not topics (BeastBoyShub model in quality-bar.md). Language: Hinglish.

## What they get from HybridBanda
Real experiments with real numbers: building businesses from Kanpur, AI side-hustle tests,
fitness/self-building, honest failures. The protagonist promise: **"I test ideas through
business, AI and storytelling — and document what actually happens."**

## Content mix (from goal.md / archive, still in force)
~70% core experiments (business, AI, creator journey) · ~20% passion pieces (fitness,
anime-inspired, travel, filmmaking) · ~10% trends/wildcards. Every video passes the
quality-bar.md checklist.

## What the channel is NEVER
1. **Never a faceless explainer/commentary channel** — no desk-narrator formats, no
   outrage/alarmism packaging (the explicit Dhruv Rathee rejection). Atishay is the
   protagonist doing the thing, not a commentator reporting it.
2. **Never generic AI-news / "Top 5 tools" content** — AI appears only inside a real
   experiment with real implementation and honest results (Master_Context content rule).
3. **Never manufactured stakes or inflated numbers** — no fake "I quit everything"
   narratives, no exaggerated revenue claims; imperfect evidence over polished fiction.
4. **Never motivational-quote / preach-first content** — lessons come out of documented
   action, never as standalone sermons.

## Success metric for audience (from goal.md)
Not follower count. The test: do people recognize and remember HIM and his work — would
they seek him out even off-platform? Leading indicators: retention, shares, meaningful
comments, inbound opportunities, enquiries tagged "came from content."

## Note for operators
The HybridBanda audience (aspiring builders) is NOT the client audience (clinic owners,
local business owners — see client-icp.md). Content serves clients as proof-of-work
documentation, but it is written for the viewer above. Don't turn the channel into an
agency brochure — the archive doc warns about this explicitly.
