# Client ICP — Who buys the retainer

Synthesized from track-record.md, goal.md, market research, July 2026. The first 30
qualified conversations refine this; the document doesn't.

## The buyer (ideal customer profile)
**An expert-led local/service business where the owner IS the face**, one converted
customer is worth thousands of rupees, enquiries already trickle in, and content is
inconsistent or ugly. Decision-maker: the owner/doctor directly (no procurement,
one-meeting closes possible — in person).

### Priority segments (evidence-ordered)
1. **Creators/experts needing AI-video production** (PROVEN: Dipankar pays ₹5–7K/video,
   repeat) — remote, referral-driven, cash now
2. **Real estate marketers/agencies** (PROVEN: Reshma retainer; Kanpur agencies validate
   the vertical) — volume reels buyers, project-launch spikes
3. **Clinics & doctors** (WARM: employment exposure + clinic-manager contact; zero
   personally-closed paid instances yet) — highest LTV, in-person trust required,
   compliance-aware content is the wedge (compliance-healthcare.md)
4. **Venues/hospitality** (RR Garden as Client Zero) — case-study vertical, father's Meta
   Ads ask

### Qualification checklist (all must be true)
- [ ] Owner/expert willing to be on camera (or has product/property to film)
- [ ] One customer worth ≥ ₹2,000 to them (clinic consult, property lead, event booking)
- [ ] Already gets SOME enquiries (we amplify, we don't create from zero)
- [ ] Can pay ≥ ₹15K/month without it being their whole marketing budget
- [ ] Reachable in person (Kanpur/Lucknow) OR came warm via referral (remote OK)

### Disqualifiers (walk away politely)
- Wants "viral" guarantees or pays per-view
- Wants full ads management with fee tied to ad results (ads = separate spend, see
  market-pricing.md rule 3)
- Haggling below the floor after seeing samples
- Big brands / procurement processes (archive doc: Phase 4, not now)

## Where they are found
In-person: clinic clusters, real-estate offices, venue owners in Kanpur (test per
operating-rhythm.md resolution rule); Lucknow day-trips as fallback. Remote: referrals
from the 5 warm threads + delivered-work referral asks (the ONLY historically proven
channel).

## What they actually buy (say this, not the tool list)
"Consistent, professional short video every month that makes you look like the obvious
choice in your area — without you hiring a team or learning any of this." For clinics add:
"—and without risking your registration" (compliance wedge).
