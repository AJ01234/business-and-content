**Cons:** Hard if background has moving things (fans, leaves, traffic).  

---

## **2. The Edited “True Freeze” Method** *(Post-Production)*
This is more like how they do it in animation — freeze *everything* but keep you moving.
1. Film the normal action.  
2. Pick the moment to “freeze.”

4. Add a *slight zoom-in* and voiceover.  

**Example workflow (CapCut / Premiere Pro):**  
- Split clip at freeze moment.  
- Turn the background into an image.  
- Mask your moving version over it.  
- Drop saturation on the frozen layer so you “pop” in color.
