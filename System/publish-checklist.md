# Publish Checklist — 10 minutes, same day as export

## Caption formula (fill, don't compose)
Line 1 = the hook, re-worded (people read it before unmuting)
Line 2 = the real number or stake ("4 rooms. ₹1cr loan. Episode 1.")
Line 3 = series tag + honest pull ("Saving My Father's Guest House — Ep 1. Follow for
the real numbers.")
Hashtags: 3–5 max, reused per series (keep a fixed set per series here after post #1):
- S1 set: ___
- S2 set: ___
- S3 set: ___

## Post (5 min)
- [ ] Cover frame chosen (face + tension, readable at thumbnail size)
- [ ] Post to Instagram Reels + YouTube Shorts (same file, both, always — no platform debates)
- [ ] Title on Shorts = hook, under 60 characters

## After posting (5 min, then CLOSE THE APP)
- [ ] Reply to comments once same evening, once next morning — then stop checking
- [ ] Any business enquiry in DMs → tag source "content" in the pipeline sheet
      (sales-pipeline.md, Known Gap #6 measurement)
- [ ] Do NOT watch the view counter. Numbers get looked at ONCE a week, in the tracker.

## The one metric ritual (Sunday, in weekly-tracker.md)
Record: views, avg watch %, saves, shares, comments-worth-reading, enquiries. One row,
five minutes, no dashboards. Judgment about the FORMAT waits until 50 posts
(past-attempts.md rule); judgment about a single video never happens.
