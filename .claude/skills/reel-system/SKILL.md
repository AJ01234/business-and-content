# Running the Reel System

Written 2026-07-12. Ground truth: `system/` (10 files — 8 reel-cycle files plus two
sales assets, offer-sheet.md and message-templates.md, covered by the sales skills, not
this one) + `context/past-attempts.md`,
`context/operating-rhythm.md`. The system targets **≤20 minutes of Atishay's involvement
per weekly cycle** for research + scripting; shoot/edit are human but timeboxed.

## Why the system is shaped this way (do not "improve" it casually)
His first run shipped 120+ videos but died of (a) low views and (b) perfection spirals —
his own words: "I tend to overdo stuff because of adhd and chasing that perfect shot,
script or edit" (interview, July 2026). Every step therefore starts from a filled
template, never a blank page, and every human step has a hard cap.

## The cycle, step by step
1. **Pick (5 min, Atishay only):** open `system/idea-bank.md`, take the top-scored READY
   idea per active series (S1 guest house / S2 AI-vs-business / S3 passion). No
   deliberation beyond the scores. New ideas get added anytime but picked only here.
2. **Research (AI, 0 min human):** paste `system/research-prompt.md` into any capable AI,
   fill the 3 blanks (idea line, 3–6 TRUE facts from context/, what happens on camera).
   ONE run; max one re-run and only if facts were missing. Output: 3 hooks, 4-beat spine,
   proof moment, scope cut, 6 b-roll shots, caption angle.
3. **Script (AI drafts, Atishay punches up 12 min for 2 reels):** AI fills
   `system/script-template.md` from the research output. Atishay's punch-up pass is
   defined in that file: say the hook aloud and rewrite it as spoken; add ONE line only
   he could say; cut explain-y sentences; check the four NEVERs. Then STOP.
4. **Log (3 min):** one row in `system/weekly-tracker.md`.
5. **Shoot:** `system/shoot-checklist.md`. 30-minute timer, hook first, 3 takes max,
   proof moment captured as a real object/screen, 6 b-roll shots. Gear is fixed: S25
   Ultra + ₹2K mic + tripod (context/tools.md).
6. **Edit:** `system/edit-checklist.md`. Two 45-min sessions max, ever. Session 2 fixes
   only the ONE gap noted in session 1. Export = done.
7. **Publish:** `system/publish-checklist.md`. Caption formula, IG Reels + YT Shorts
   both, enquiry source-tagging, then close the app.

## "Done correctly" looks like
- 2 reels shipped in the week; tracker row filled Sunday. Cycle minutes ≤20 is the DESIGN
  TARGET (never yet measured in practice — recalibrate against the first 4 logged cycles;
  ≤30 real minutes is still a win vs. the old hours-long process)
- Zero mid-week view-checking; metrics recorded once, Sunday
- No reel got a third edit session; no idea was re-picked after selection
- Each shipped reel passes the 5-point gate in `context/quality-bar.md`

## Common failure smells → named causes
- Cycle minutes >30 two weeks running → re-running prompts or re-picking ideas (both banned)
- A blank tracker row → the bad-week tripwire: "consuming content and planning instead of
  selling or pitching or doing things" (his words). Response: shrink next action to 15 min.
- Judging the format before post #50 → forbidden (past-attempts.md rule).

## When NOT to use this skill
Not for CLIENT deliverables (client work follows offer.md scope + the simpler client bar
in quality-bar.md, not the HybridBanda artistic loop). Not for long-form documentaries
(only 1/month required; treat separately). Not for redesigning the system inside a
production week — improvements happen at 90-day reviews.

How to re-verify: read the last 4 rows of system/weekly-tracker.md — if cycle minutes,
ship rate, and rules hold, the system is alive as documented.
