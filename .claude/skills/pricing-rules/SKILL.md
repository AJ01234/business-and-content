# Pricing Rules

Written 2026-07-12. Ground truth: `context/offer.md`, `context/market-pricing.md`,
`context/track-record.md`, `goal.md`.

## Why this skill exists (the wound)
His documented pricing history: quoted ₹80 for a project; ₹800 for a banner; half-paid
for editing; ₹15–20K for 25–30 videos (≈₹500–700/video). Underpricing is the single most
repeated failure in the record. Prices here are WRITTEN LAW, not suggestions.

## The floors (absolute, from goal.md + offer.md)
- No project below **₹5,000**
- No retainer below **₹15,000/month**
- **50% upfront**, 2 revision rounds max, scope in writing before work starts
- Never quote below ≈₹1,500 per short-form video equivalent inside any bundle

## The package ladder (draft v1, offer.md — validate contents against real capacity)
| Package | Contents (draft) | Floor |
|---|---|---|
| Starter | 8 shorts/mo, scripts+hooks, 2 revisions | ₹15,000/mo |
| Growth | 12–15 shorts + 1 AI long-form, posting guidance, report | ₹25,000/mo |
| Authority | 15+ shorts + 2 AI videos + shoot-day + repurposing | ₹35,000+/mo |
| Per-project AI video | Dipankar-style one-off | ₹5,000–10,000 |
Anchor conversations at Growth (₹25K) so Starter feels accessible (market-pricing.md).
⚠️ The FLOORS are law; the CONTENTS (video counts per tier) are unvalidated against his
real delivery capacity until the first client month is time-tracked (context/tools.md).
Adjust counts if margins demand; never adjust floors. Printable version:
`system/offer-sheet.md`.

## Why the floors are DEFENSIBLE (say this under pressure)
India market benchmarks (researched July 2026, context/market-pricing.md): reels
₹500–3,000 each; small-business social retainers typically ₹15–50K/month; ₹25–35K/month
freelance retainers are commonly cited. His floor is bottom-third of normal — a balking
prospect has a trust objection, not a price objection. Answer with proof (samples, case
study), NEVER with a number below floor.

## Non-negotiable mechanics
- Ad spend is ALWAYS separate from fees (industry norm; protects fee from "ads didn't
  work" blame — market-pricing.md rule 3)
- Existing warm clients get repriced UP to floor on next engagement (Dipankar's
  ₹5–7K/video is already fair — hold or raise; Reshma's old ₹500–700/video is DEAD,
  requote at retainer floor)
- Growth-systems upsell pilot: ~₹10K first pilot (goal.md Known Gap #5), only after the
  upsell gate passes
- First ₹15K retainer's spend priority: legalize the software stack (context/tools.md)

## Discount pressure playbook
1. Restate the deliverable value in THEIR numbers (one patient/deal covers the month)
2. Offer to REDUCE SCOPE (Starter instead of Growth), never the rate
3. Silence after quoting. He fills silences with discounts — the printed sheet exists so
   the paper holds the number when nerves don't.
4. Walk-away is allowed and budgeted for: the ICP disqualifiers include hagglers
   (context/client-icp.md).
