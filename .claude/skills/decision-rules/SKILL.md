# Decision Rules & Non-Negotiables

Written 2026-07-12. Ground truth: `goal.md` (Decision rules + Constraints),
`context/operating-rhythm.md`, `context/finances.md`, interviews July 2026.

## The five decision rules (goal.md, verbatim intent + WHY)
1. **Runway rule:** cash < 2 months of expenses → take paid work immediately, rebuild
   after. WHY: runway is ~3 months from July 2026 on his own conservative assumptions
   (ends ~mid-Oct 2026, UNVERIFIED — context/finances.md); denial is the historical
   pattern, so the trigger is mechanical.
2. **Day-90 gate:** zero paying retainers at proper price by Day 90 (~Oct 9, 2026) →
   diagnose from data: too few pitches? pitched-not-closed? closed-but-underpriced? Fix
   the NAMED failure. Direction changes only after 20+ recorded conversations say the
   offer is wrong. WHY: he historically pivots on mood/one rejection.
3. **Upsell gate:** growth-systems pitched only after his retainer ran ≥1 month at that
   client with a number that improved. WHY: zero paid proof exists for that service.
4. **Content guardrail:** miss both weekly videos 3 weeks running → reduce scope
   elsewhere; content hours are protected but never excuse skipped outreach. WHY: content
   is his love and his escape hatch — both truths are managed, not denied.
5. **One experiment at a time:** new ideas are logged and ignored 90 days unless they
   serve the current offer. WHY: identity-switching is failure pattern #4.

## Money non-negotiables
- Price floors: ≥₹5,000/project, ≥₹15,000/month retainer, 50% upfront, 2 revision rounds
  (context/offer.md). WHY: underpricing is his most documented failure.
- Budget cap ₹30,000; contractors only against paid work; ad SPEND always separate from
  fees (context/market-pricing.md rule 3). WHY: protects fee from "ads didn't work" blame.

## Time non-negotiables (38–40 h/week, goal.md)
~20h selling+delivery · 8h protected HybridBanda · 6–8h RR Garden CAP · 4h admin.
Personal, untouchable (his words, Q10): **gym, sleep pattern, food on time** — never
schedule against them; they stabilize the ADHD pattern.
RR Garden: exceeds its cap 3 weeks running → renegotiate scope with father, don't absorb
(context/rr-garden.md). Paint shop: zero hours this year.

## Standing priorities when rules collide (inference, from goal.md's structure)
Survival cash > first-client milestones > client delivery > HybridBanda cadence >
RR Garden > everything else. A rule lower on the list never overrides one higher.

## When NOT to use this skill
Not a substitute for judgment on NEW situations the rules don't cover — then: check
failure-archaeology, check context/, and if genuinely novel, ask Atishay. Don't use the
rules to avoid action ("no rule covers it" ≠ "don't do it").

How to re-verify: rules live in goal.md — diff this skill against goal.md at every 90-day
review; goal.md wins on any conflict.
