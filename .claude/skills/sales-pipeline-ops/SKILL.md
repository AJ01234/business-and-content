# Sales Pipeline Operations

Written 2026-07-12. Ground truth: `context/sales-pipeline.md`, `context/client-icp.md`,
`context/track-record.md`, interviews July 2026.

## The structural fact
The ENTIRE warm network is 5 threads (confirmed complete, July 2026): Dipankar Biswas
(HOT — returned ~late June 2026 with a new song), Reshma (real estate, pitched him 2–3x
to return), the aesthetic-clinic manager (once tried to hire him), Abhishek + referral
circle, the Palava WhatsApp group. Five threads cannot yield 20 conversations/month →
in-person pitching + referral asks are structural, not optional.

## Definitions (zero-context reader)
- **Qualified conversation:** decision-maker + their content/leads discussed + past-spend
  question asked + concrete next step proposed. A WhatsApp "hi" does not count.
- **Discovery core question (past behaviour, never hypotheticals):** "What did you spend
  on marketing/content last quarter? Who did it? What did you get?" Never "would you buy?"

## The follow-up cadence (the anti-fear machine — sending is mechanical, not emotional)
Day 0 pitch → Day 2 value follow-up → Day 7 nudge → Day 14 "closing the loop" → recycle
to 60-day dormant list. The actual message texts — warm-thread openers for all 5 threads,
the 3 cadence follow-ups, and the in-person meeting booker — live in
`system/message-templates.md` (drafts; Atishay punches up each on first use and the
improved version replaces the draft there).

## Activity targets (goal.md)
10 in-person pitches/month from Month 1; 20 qualified conversations/month by Month 3
(Oct 2026); first paid retainer at proper price by Day 30 (~Aug 10, 2026). Sunday
scorecard: pitches held, proposals sent, follow-ups done. 3 zero-weeks → Decision Rule 2
diagnosis.

## In-person doctrine (his own field lesson)
"Indian audience… don't trust when we call. It's better if we pitch them in person
physically" — from his Desire Edits cold-calling. Calls BOOK meetings; meetings CLOSE.
Doctors especially. Bring: printed 3-package sheet (pricing-rules skill), phone with 2–3
work samples, the discovery questions. His Kanpur-pessimism is an untested belief with a
resolution rule (context/operating-rhythm.md): 10 documented pitches decide, then
Lucknow day-trips + remote/referral if truly dead.

## Referral engine (the only historically proven channel)
Every past client came via referral or his own digging — zero from content, zero from
cold. Therefore: every delivered project ENDS with a direct ask for exactly two
introductions. This is a checklist step, not a mood.

## "Done correctly" looks like
Every warm thread has a dated next action; no lead sits >2 days without its cadence step;
the Sunday row is filled; every enquiry has a source tag (content/referral/in-person) —
that tag is the Known-Gap-#6 measurement.

## When NOT to use this skill
Not for pricing/package decisions (pricing-rules skill). Not for delivery once closed
(offer.md scope). Don't use pipeline busywork (list-polishing, CRM decorating) as
substitute for sending the next message — that's the documented avoidance pattern.

How to re-verify: open the pipeline sheet; if ≥1 thread has no dated next action or the
last Sunday row is blank, the pipeline is NOT being run as designed.
