# The Hardest Open Problem: First Paid Retainer vs. The Runway

Written 2026-07-12. Ground truth: `goal.md` milestones, `context/finances.md`,
`context/sales-pipeline.md`, `context/track-record.md`, `context/operating-rhythm.md`.

## Why THIS is the hardest problem (stated, not assumed)
Runway ends ~mid-Oct 2026 (assumed figures he chose; unverified). The Day-30 milestone
(~Aug 10, 2026) demands the first properly-priced retainer. Working against it: 5 warm
threads total, zero clients ever from cold or content, his Kanpur-market pessimism
(untested), and the documented avoidance pattern ("consuming content and planning
instead of selling"). Working for it: Dipankar returned UNASKED with a new song (~late
June 2026); Reshma has repeatedly asked him to work with her again (2–3 times — timing
not dated); he's proven in-person capable (stalls, cold calls at Desire Edits); and
prices are already set in writing.

## The sequence (highest probability first — evidence-ordered)
**Move 1 — Dipankar (days 1–7).** Active demand exists NOW. Quote the new song at
₹5–7K+ (his established fair rate — hold or raise, never below). Deliver via the AI-video
workflow (context/tools.md). On delivery: ask for exactly two introductions (the referral
engine — his only proven channel). This is the fastest possible cash + a portfolio piece.
**Move 2 — Reshma (days 1–14).** She asked him back 2–3 times. Re-open with the retainer
sheet: Starter ₹15K/8 videos — a fraction of her old 25–30/mo volume at a proper price.
The old ₹500–700/video rate is dead; if she balks, reduce scope, never rate
(pricing-rules skill).
**Move 3 — The clinic manager (days 7–21, IN PERSON).** She once tried to HIRE him.
Propose the project-basis retainer instead. Compliance-aware clinic content
(compliance-healthcare context) is the differentiator no local shop pitches.
**Move 4 — The Kanpur test (days 14–45).** 10 documented in-person pitches (clinics,
real-estate offices, venues from context/client-icp.md). This settles his "Kanpur has no
market" belief with data (context/operating-rhythm.md resolution rule). If 10 real
pitches say dead: Lucknow day-trips + remote referral doubling — pre-decided, no
mid-course despair required. Bring: the printed `system/offer-sheet.md`, phone with 2–3
samples, the discovery questions; openers and follow-ups come from
`system/message-templates.md`.
**Parallel — RR Garden + HybridBanda** continue inside their caps: the enquiry system
becomes the growth-systems demo; the reels document the journey (S2 series is literally
this plan).

## The failure modes to police (from his record, not imagination)
1. Polishing assets instead of sending messages (7-day sell-before-build rule, goal.md)
2. Quoting under floor when nervous (bring the printed sheet; silence after quoting)
3. Skipping follow-ups (the Day 0/2/7/14 cadence is mechanical — sales-pipeline-ops)
4. Reading one "no" as market proof (Decision Rule 2: 20+ recorded conversations decide)

## Checkpoints
- Day 30 (~Aug 10, 2026): ≥1 paid retainer at floor+ OR a written diagnosis of which
  named failure occurred — not a mood, a mechanism.
- Day 90 (~Oct 9, 2026): ≥₹25K/month collected (survival floor) or the Day-90 gate
  diagnosis runs. Cash below 2 months of expenses at ANY point → Runway rule: take paid
  work immediately, no shame spiral (Decision Rule 1).

## When NOT to use this skill
After 3+ stable retainer clients exist, this skill is history — switch to scaling
questions (capacity, contractors, upsell). Also not for HybridBanda growth problems
(that's reel-system + quality-bar territory).
