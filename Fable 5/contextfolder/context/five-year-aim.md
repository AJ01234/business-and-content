# Five-Year Aim — The layer above goal.md

Source: Atishay's own "Me in 5 Years" YouTube video, recorded 21 April 2026 (no-cut,
~22 min, from his Mumbai flat), transcript provided July 2026. This file states the
long-horizon aim; goal.md remains the 12-month operating goal and is NOT changed by this.

## The aim, in his own words (translated/condensed from the transcript)
- **By ~April 2031 (age 29): a cult audience of 100K–150K subscribers on YouTube.**
  Verbatim intent: "आई होप आई हैव समवेयर अराउंड 100 के सब्सक्राइबर्स… 100 के 150 के
  सब्सक्राइबर इज़ अ गुड अमाउंट." (His July 2026 message paraphrased this as "100k or
  200k" — the recorded video says 100–150K; treat 100K as the anchor.)
- **Cult audience defined by him:** people who stand by you whatever happens — even 500
  or 1,000 true fans would satisfy him ("500 की कल्ट ऑडियंस… भी मुझे चलेगी"). Scale is
  desired; loyalty is the non-negotiable.
- **Stay in the content space, period:** "मैं चाहता हूं मैं कंटेंट क्रिएशन में ही रहूं…
  कंटेंट क्रिएटर हूं या फिर कोई कंटेंट बिज़नेस रन कर रहा हूं। इन दो चीजों के अराउंड ही
  मेरे को रहना है। और यही मेरा ऐम है." Creator OR content-business owner — nothing
  outside those two.
- **Money philosophy (his words):** not chasing millionaire status or show-off money.
  Enough to pursue dreams, be financially free, send money home, and invest to protect
  the family's future. "पैसा वाज़ नेवर द इंस्पिरेशन… व्हाट इंस्पायर्ड मी वाज़ कि आई
  रियली वांटेड दैट कल्ट ऑडियंस. आई रियली वांटेड दैट फेम."
- **The deeper driver:** childhood as "that average person who gets ignored" — the
  introvert who built a studious persona; recognition and connection are the fuel, not
  luxury. He wants to "give back" MrBeast-style to whoever stands with him.

## His own strategy articulation (important — he already believes the model)
From the transcript, unprompted: shorts/reels earn momentary traction and a small local
audience → funnel to long-form → and the Flying Beast model: "यू गो इन वन थिंग, यू डिग
दैट डीप, एंड देन यू शिफ्ट इट टुवर्ड्स अदर थिंग्स" — go deep in ONE thing first, earn the
core audience, THEN expand to everything else. He dislikes niches but he himself
articulated that breadth is EARNED through depth. Any future coaching on "one lens first"
should quote his own words back to him, not argue theory. He also explicitly rejects
clickbait/attention-hacking: "आई डोंट वांट जस्ट अट्रैक्शन… आई वांट टू बिल्ड अ रियल
कनेक्शन" (OG references: Bhuvan Bam, Tanmay Bhatt, Samay Raina).

## How this connects to goal.md (do not blur them)
goal.md (₹1L/month by July 2027) is the FUNDING MECHANISM for this aim: income buys the
freedom to create without desperation, and the business experiments ARE the content. The
5-year subscriber aim never overrides goal.md's rule that followers are a secondary
metric in year 1 — 100K by 2031 does not require virality in 2026; it requires the
cadence + format fix (past-attempts.md) sustained for years.

## Sanity math (labeled inference)
180 → 100,000 in ~5 years is a 555x. Not linear-feasible; channels reach it via a small
number of breakout videos on top of consistent cadence. Operational translation: ship the
weekly system (reel-system), give every video a real hook, and let long-form documentaries
(his GetSetFly ambition) be the breakout surface. No subscriber targets before post #50.
