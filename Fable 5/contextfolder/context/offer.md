# Offer — What we sell, in what order

Derived from goal.md and track-record.md. v1 (July 2026); package contents to be confirmed
against Atishay's delivery capacity (see interview) and market-pricing.md.

## Entry offer (the ONLY thing sold from Day 1)
**Productized monthly video retainer** — short-form content + AI-assisted video for
expert-led local businesses (doctors/clinics, real estate professionals, creators/experts
who have already paid him).

Draft package ladder (prices are floors, not targets — validate against market-pricing.md):

| Package | Contents (draft) | Price floor |
|---|---|---|
| Starter | 8 short-form videos/mo, scripts + hooks included, 2 revision rounds | ₹15,000/mo |
| Growth | 12–15 shorts + 1 AI long-form video/mo, posting guidance, monthly report | ₹25,000/mo |
| Authority | 15+ shorts + 2 AI videos + shoot day support + repurposing | ₹35,000+/mo |
| Per-project AI video | One-off AI-generated/edited video (Dipankar-style) | ₹5,000–10,000/video |

Hard rules: no project below ₹5,000; no retainer below ₹15,000/month; 50% upfront;
2 revision rounds max; scope in writing before work starts.

## Upsell (earned, never led with)
**Content-to-conversion system**: enquiry log, WhatsApp follow-up cadence, CRM improvement,
source tracking, monthly report. Pitched only to a client whose retainer has run ≥1 month
with a number that improved (goal.md Decision Rule 3). First paid pilot target: ~₹10K
(Known Gap #5). RR Garden is the live demo (rr-garden.md).

## Long-term differentiator (portfolio track, not a business yet)
AI films / branded visual storytelling. Max 2–3 spec pieces per quarter, built from real
client work. Becomes a service line only when someone pays for one.

## Positioning sentence (client-facing)
"I make consistent, high-quality short-form and AI video for your practice/business every
month — so you show up professionally without hiring a team." Outcome language, not
tool language. The growth-systems pitch appears only after delivery has earned trust.

## What we do NOT sell (for now)
- Websites, SEO, paid-ads management, branding packages
- CRM implementation as a standalone (upsell only)
- Wedding photography/videography agency services (see archive doc; revisit only via
  RR Garden pilot with ≥5 packages sold)
- "AI consulting" or tools training
